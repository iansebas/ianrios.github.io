What is #closr?

#closr is a platform that allows you to:

  • Use hashtags to search
  • Look who's around
  • Message securely with end-to-end encryption
  • Share your social media
  • Networks and form communities around you
  • Connect creatively and more!

Create a profile and start browsing!

There is no only one "correct" way to use #closr.

Get #gym buddies or #salsa #dancing partners, showcase your #fashion style, find a chemistry #studygroup , invite people to #party, make friends in the #lgbtq community, be the #mentor to a younger #entrepreneur, give #dining recommendations,...

Literally anything. Be #creative!
